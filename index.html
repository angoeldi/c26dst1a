Consent

You are invited to take part in a short research study about how onboarding text in AI chat interfaces affects judgments. It takes about 1–2 minutes.

  • What you will do: read a short onboarding banner in a chat UI and answer a few questions.
  • Voluntary: participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time before submitting by closing this page.
  • Withdrawal after submitting: you can request deletion of your responses by messaging the researchers via Prolific with your Prolific ID within 30 days. If the data have already been irreversibly anonymized, deletion may not be possible.
  • Data collected: your Prolific participant ID (for payment, de-duplication, and withdrawal requests), your responses, and basic timestamps (time spent). We do not ask for your name, and we do not intentionally record your IP address.
  • Confidentiality: data are stored in a private research spreadsheet and accessed only by the research team. Results will be reported in aggregate; we will not publish your Prolific ID.
  • Data storage and retention: data are stored in Google Sheets (Google LLC). We will keep the dataset for up to 5 years for research and auditing purposes, then delete it. Identifiers (Prolific ID) will be removed from analysis files as soon as practical.
  • Risks: minimal risk. Some people may find questions about AI rights or harm uncomfortable. You may stop at any time.
  • Compensation: as described in the Prolific study listing, for completed submissions.
  • Incomplete disclosure: to preserve the integrity of the study, we cannot describe all details in advance. You will receive a short debrief at the end.
  • Questions: contact the researchers via Prolific messages. For platform or payment issues, contact Prolific Support.
  • Eligibility: by continuing, you confirm you are at least 18 years old.
